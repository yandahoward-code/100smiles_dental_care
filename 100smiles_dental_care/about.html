Our Story

Located at 33 Fantail Street, Razole, 100SMILES Dental Care has proudly served the community for over three decades. We are committed to delivering compassionate, affordable, and high-quality dental care.

Our team specialises in preventive, restorative, and cosmetic dentistry. Whether it's routine check-ups, dental implants, emergency treatment, or orthodontics your smile is always our priority.
